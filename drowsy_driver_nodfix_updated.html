👓 Wearing Glasses?
Works with most prescription glasses — MediaPipe tracks eye landmarks under the lens, not the frame. Avoid glare: angle the camera/light so lenses don't reflect bright light directly into the camera, as reflections can hide the eyes. Thick dark sunglasses block eye tracking entirely — switch to clear or photochromic lenses while driving with this system active.
